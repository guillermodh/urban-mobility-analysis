# Urban Mobility vs Economic Productivity

## Objective
Analyze the relationship between urban mobility (travel time and congestion) and economic productivity (GDP per capita) across major Latin American cities.

## Tools
Python, Pandas

## Process
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Correlation analysis between mobility and economic indicators

## Key Insights
- Cities with higher congestion tend to show lower economic productivity
- Travel time is a key factor impacting economic performance

## Conclusion
Urban mobility plays an important role in economic outcomes, suggesting that improving transportation systems could positively impact productivity.
